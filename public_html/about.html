About Simpson College Football

A Storied Tradition

Conference Championships
1923, 1924, 1925, 1931, 1933, 1934, 1949, 1969, 1988, 1991, 1996, 1997
NCAA Postseason Appearances
1988, 1989, 1991, 1996, 1997, 2003

All Americans

22 All Americans all time

Some First Team All Americans include Ricky Gales, Chris Winey, Brent Parrott, and Jeremy Whalen. There were 4 players who were 2-Time All Americans. Those players were Jason Ripke, Mike Donnenwerth, Guy Leman, and Clint Head. There were a few All-Time All-Americans. One of those players, Jason Ripke, was First-Team All American, 2-Time All American, and All-Time All American as a defensive lineman/defensive end in 2004 and 2005.

Academic Excellence

27 academic All-Conference players in the last two years.

Why Simpson?

  • Great Location
    • Simpson is located just south of Des Moines. Both Indianola and Des Moines offer plenty of places to eat and fun activities.
  • Campus Life
    • Simpson has a tight knit campus community and there is always something to do right here, on campus
  • Storm Athletics
    • Obviously football is the reason you are viewing this page, but the athletics department runs deep with many sports represented. Watching other Storm teams compete at a high level is another advantage of coming to Simpson College.
