Simpson College Football Facilities

Steven Johnson Fitness Center

Opened to Simpson Students January of 2014

This was a $6 Million Project that has two levels both over 4,000 Square Foot

It is open to all students, but the lower level is mainly used by athletes

Hours

Monday – Thursday: 6 a.m. – 10 p.m.

Friday: 6 a.m. – 8 p.m.

Saturday: 10 a.m. – 4 p.m.

Sunday closed

The weight room is a great place to get better as an athlete and a football player. Whether it's weight training, conditioning, mobility, or stretching, the Fitness Center has it all. With good equipment, actively involved trainers, and lots of space, athletes get a great place to work on their skills that will transfer to the field.

Bill Buxton Stadium

Opened: 1949

Renamed: 1998

Capacity: 5,000

Surface: FieldTurf Revolution

Football, Soccer, and Track and Field will all, used this stadium
