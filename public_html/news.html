This Season

Storm Picked to Finish 5th in the American Rivers Conference (ARC) Preseason poll

The Storm were picked to finish 5th in the ARC by the preseason coaches' poll

The Storm are off to a rough start to the season. They are current 0-1 in the conference with a 84-16 loss to Central. They are also 0-4 on the season, losing to Mary Hardin-Baylor 84-6, Wisconsin-Stevens Point 34-7, and Coe 42-22. The Storm look to bounce back next week against Loras College.

The Storm Face Three Top 25 Teams on Their Schedule This Year

Simpson has plenty of opportunities to prove it this season with three preseason top 25 teams on their schedule. These teams include Mary-Hardin Baylor, Central College, and Wartburg College.

The Simpson Storm had a rough two games against Mary-Hardin Baylor and Central. Losing 84-6 and 84-16. These two teams showed why they are ranked so high in the standings. Simpson hopes to compete against Wartburg later in the season.

Conference Play

Conference play begins for the Storm September 18th as they travel to Coe College to play the Kohawks in the first conference game of the year for both teams.

The Storm are moving on to conference play as a postseason spot is on the line for the winner of the American Rivers Conference.
